# LinkedIn Skills Video — AgentForge

## Style Prompt

Swiss Pulse with a modern product-launch edge: precise, technical, and high-clarity. Square canvas optimized for LinkedIn feed. Structured grid blocks, strong typographic hierarchy, and directional transitions that communicate progress from problem to capability to outcomes. Visual tone is confident and engineering-led.

## Colors

- `#0d1117` — primary background
- `#f3f6fb` — primary foreground text
- `#19a0ff` — accent blue for highlights and transitions
- `#1f2937` — panel/background surfaces
- `#7a8a9f` — secondary metadata text

## Typography

- **Archivo Black** (900) for headlines and key numbers
- **JetBrains Mono** (400/700) for metadata, labels, skills, and technical cues

## Motion

- Scene entrances use `expo.out`, `power3.out`, `back.out(1.8)`, and `sine.out`.
- Transitions are directional slide-crossfades (primary), with one stronger wipe at the mid-point.
- No exit animations before transitions; final scene fades to black only at the end.

## What NOT to Do

- No purple/cyan gradient backgrounds.
- No generic centered text-only slides with empty space.
- No tiny text (<20px body) — readability on mobile feed is required.
- No repeated identical entrance pattern for all elements.
- No decorative clutter unrelated to skills narrative.

## Narrative (24s)

1. Hook — “Skills I learned building AgentForge”
2. Architecture mindset — orchestration model and role routing
3. Backend & infra skills — Python, queueing, workflows, APIs
4. Platform & deployment skills — Docker, Kubernetes, Terraform, observability
5. AI product skills — prompts, eval loops, reliability, iteration
6. Closing CTA — “Building in public” + handle placeholder
